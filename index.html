Frequently Asked Questions

Do you handle full renovations and new builds? +
Yes — it's our specialty. We take care of the complete electrical fit-out for new builds and renovations, including kitchens, bathrooms, lighting, power points, data and rewiring, planned with you and completed on budget.
Can you install data cabling and home automation? +
Absolutely. We install wired Ethernet and data points, networking, TV points and home automation — ideal for new builds and renovations, and a tidy fix for homes with patchy Wi-Fi.
How much does a switchboard upgrade cost in Hamilton? +
Most residential switchboard upgrades range from about $1,500 to $5,000 depending on the size of the board, RCD requirements and the condition of your wiring. We always provide a fixed quote before any work begins.
Do you offer 24/7 emergency call-outs? +
Yes. We provide fast 24/7 emergency electrician call-outs across Hamilton and the Waikato for power faults, tripping, sparking and switchboard failures. Call us any time and we'll get someone to you.
Which areas do you cover? +
We're based in Western Heights, Hamilton and serve Dinsdale, Chartwell, Hillcrest, Fairfield, Glenview, Hamilton Lake, Cambridge, Tamahere and across Waipa and the South Waikato.
